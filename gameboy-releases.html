Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree Edition

Release Date: 21 June 2024

"Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree" is an eagerly awaited action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware in collaboration with acclaimed fantasy author George R.R. Martin. Set in a vast and intricately designed open-world, players embark on a perilous journey to unravel the mysteries of the Elden Ring, a powerful artifact that has been shattered, plunging the world into chaos. With a focus on challenging combat, deep exploration, and rich storytelling, players can expect to encounter daunting enemies, uncover hidden lore, and forge their own path through a dark and atmospheric fantasy realm.

Destiny 2: The Final Shape

Release Date: 4 June 2024

"Destiny 2: The Final Shape" is an expansion for the popular online multiplayer shooter, Destiny 2. In this expansion, players confront the ultimate threat to the universe as they face off against the enigmatic forces known as the Darkness. With new story missions, challenges, and rewards, players must rally together to uncover the secrets of the ancient Darkness and prevent its cataclysmic plans from coming to fruition. This expansion promises to introduce fresh gameplay mechanics, formidable enemies, and epic moments as players fight for the fate of the galaxy.
